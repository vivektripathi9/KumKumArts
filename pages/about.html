About the retreat

A space to slow down and create freely.

This 3-day retreat blends pottery, painting, nature, and mindful living in the serene beauty of Bir.

What is this retreat about?

This is a 3-day immersive art and mindfulness retreat designed to help you slow down, reconnect, and create freely in the mountains.

Whether you're a beginner or someone who just wants to explore creativity again, this retreat offers a safe and inspiring space to express yourself through art.

Beginner-friendly

No prior experience required. Come as you are, and let the mountains, materials, and community guide the process.
